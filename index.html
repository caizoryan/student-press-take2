*OCADU STUDENT CHRONICLE*
Welcome to the OCADU Chronicle website!
The OCAD U Student Chronicle, also known as the OCADU Student Press, serves as a platform for idea sharing, research, and communication among the OCAD U student and faculty community. Over the recent years, the student press has been lost in the void, but is back, with the same ethos, to honour the community an the press that came before it.
